The Person In Need Project

COVID-19 is a challenging time for all of us, but especially for our vulnerable communities.

We understand that if you are a person in need (elderly, chronic conditions, disabilities, unemployed, health workers), you may not have or should not be going out to get the essential supplies you need.


We can connect you with people nearby who will help you.

Step 1: Ask for help.

Step 2: Get matched with a volunteer.

Step 3: Volunteer delivers essential supplies to your doorstep!
